Welcome

To the world of Nahian

STEAM is a better acronym than STEM. Art is science and Science is art.

I grew up reading books and listening to music. I also was pretty decent at school, a "jack of all trades, but a master of none" type of person. It was hard to pinpoint what I wanted to do and what I wanted to be, and having labels attached to my identity never made it easier for others or myself to understand that either. Once I started letting go of ideas like that "I need to be THIS", or "I am THAT", I started getting out of my comfort zone, and just doing things that piqued my curiosity. I realized that I like the science of things as much as I like the art of things. And I don't need to limit myself by being a doctor or a writer, I'm not a nerd or a jock, I'm just me. And this is what I do.
