A set of moves becomes a movement

And in turn, moves people

	
    I started dancing when I was a freshman in college, when I joined the

    "break dance" club at Union. I was trying to make friends and get out

    of my comfort zone, dancing helped with that, but it gave me something

    more. It helped me convey all the pent up emotions I ever had and let

    me be myself. It took me out of my shell and I became obsessed with an

    activity that I wanted to keep getting better at. I love it a lot.
